12 columns responsive

Example:

  • Desktop (default): 12 columns
  • Tablet landscape (md): 4 columns
  • Tablet portrait / Mobile landscape (sm): 2 columns
  • Mobile portrait (xs): 1 column

Note: Setting columns to 0, md0, sm0 or xs0 will hide the column for that breakpoint.
