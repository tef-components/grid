12 columns responsive

Example:

  • Desktop: 12 columns
  • Tablet landscape: 4 columns
  • Tablet portrait / Mobile landscape: 2 columns
  • Mobile portrait: 1 column

Note: Setting data-columns-md, data-columns-sm or data-columns-xs to 0 will hide the column for that breakpoint
